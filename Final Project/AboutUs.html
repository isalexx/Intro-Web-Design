About Our Company

Altegra Clothing was first founded in September of 2003, by Alex Shevchenko (current Chief Executive Officer) and Cole Andison (current Chief Operating Officer). It was created with the goal to sell awesome looking streetwear, with quality and affordability in mind. Stationed right here in Toronto, Ontario, we are proud to be a Canadian owned and operated business to provide products to people worldwide. Throughout the years we have achieved many of our goals thanks to our appreciated customers. Thank you for shopping at Altegra Clothing.
